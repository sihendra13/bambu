# Bamboo Fence Export Research Summary

## 🌍 Market Overview
- **Global Market Size**: $79.36B (2025) → $85.66B (2026) - Growing 7.8% annually
- **Indonesia Position**: 2.1 million hectares bamboo forest - one of world's largest reserves
- **Status**: Transitioning from commodity exporter → branded finished goods manufacturer

## 📊 Export Markets (High Demand)
1. **USA** - Residential & commercial projects (premium segment)
2. **Europe** - Garden fencing, privacy screens (established importers)
3. **Japan** - Artisan quality, traditional designs
4. **Australia, Canada, Mexico** - Growing market

## 🎋 Product Types & Specifications

### Standard Dimensions
- **Height**: 4ft, 6ft, 8ft (residential), custom for commercial
- **Width**: 8ft standard (96-120cm panels)
- **Diameter**: 0.75" - 1" (thicker poles = premium, slower growth)

### Materials
- **Tonkin Bamboo** - Premium quality, dense, durable
- **Moso Bamboo** - Natural, eco-friendly, good durability
- **Carbonized Bamboo** - Dark brown color, steam heat treated (no chemicals)

### Treatment Grades
1. **Natural Raw** - Basic, entry-level pricing
2. **Heat-Treated** (200°C) - EU standard durability class, weather resistant
3. **Fumigated + Dried** - Export-grade, pest-resistant, quality tested
4. **Carbonized Treatment** - Premium, dark color, highest durability

## 💰 Pricing Structure
- **Basic Panels**: $100-150 USD per 8ft x 4ft
- **Premium (Treated)**: $250-400 USD per panel
- **Bulk Orders**: Custom pricing (wholesale advantage)
- **Higher Diameter (8-12cm)**: 20-30% premium due to rarity

## 🏭 Key Suppliers & Positioning
- **Indonesia**: Cost-competitive, large volume capacity
- **Japan (Nagaokameichiku)**: Artisan quality, craftsmanship-focused
- **China/Vietnam**: Cost-leader but lower quality perception
- **EU Importers (Intergard, Bamboo Import)**: 40+ years experience, established distribution
