## 📊 Key Sections Details

### 1. HERO
```
Headline: "Premium Sustainable Bamboo Fencing"
Subheadline: "Export-ready bamboo panels from Indonesia's sustainable farms. 
Suitable for residential & commercial projects worldwide."

Stats:
- 2.1M hectares sustainable bamboo forest
- 500-1000 MT/month capacity (estimate)
- 15+ countries served
- 100% sustainable harvesting
```

### 2. PRODUCTS
Three product lines:

**Product A: Natural Tonkin Bamboo**
- Dimensions: 4ft/6ft/8ft height × 8ft width × 0.75-1" diameter
- Color: Natural honey-brown
- Use: Residential gardens, decorative screens
- Specs: Raw, sun-dried, premium quality
- Export Markets: USA (DIY, residential), Europe (garden centers)

**Product B: Heat-Treated Carbonized Bamboo**
- Same dimensions, dark brown color
- Treatment: 200°C heat-treated (no chemicals, EU certified)
- Durability: Highest class (15-20 year lifespan)
- Use: High-end residential, commercial landscaping
- Export Markets: USA (premium segment), Europe, Japan

**Product C: Commercial Grade Panels**
- Custom dimensions available
- Galvanized steel reinforcement
- Bulk production capability (500+ MT/month)
- Use: Residential developments, commercial projects, landscaping contractors
- Export Markets: Bulk buyers, developers

### 3. SUPPLY CAPACITY
```
Production Stats:
- Monthly Capacity: 500-1000 MT (scalable)
- Warehouse: 1000 MT storage ready
- Harvest Locations: Java, Sumatra (sustainable farms)
- Lead Time: 30-45 days for custom orders
- Shipping: Direct to major ports (Singapore, Hong Kong)
```

### 4. CERTIFICATIONS
```
- FSSC 22000 (Food Safety) or equivalent quality system
- COA (Certificate of Analysis) - moisture, durability testing
- Phytosanitary Certificate (export clearance)
- EU DIN Standard (heat-treated products)
- Sustainability Certification (eco-friendly sourcing)
```

### 5. PROCESS (5 Steps)
```
01. Harvest → From sustainable farms, selective cutting
02. Natural Dry → Sun-dry to 12-15% moisture content
03. Treatment → Heat treatment or natural raw (per product)
04. Quality Control → Inspection, testing, grading
05. Ship → Packed in containers, export-ready
```

### 6. IMPACT
```
Sustainability:
- 100% renewable resource
- Fast growth (3-5 years harvesting cycle)
- Carbon negative production
- Farm community support
```
